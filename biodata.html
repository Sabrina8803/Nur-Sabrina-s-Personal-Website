My Short Bio

My full name is Nur Sabrina binti Abdul Gahfar. I was born on August 8, 2003, and I am 21 years old in 2024. I am a native of Selangor. From the age of 7 to 12, I attended the Sekolah Rendah Agama Integrasi (SRAI) in Seksyen 19 Shah Alam, Selangor. Subsequently, I continued my education at SMK 24(2) Shah Alam, Selangor until the age of 17. After that, I pursued my studies at the Kolej Tingkatan Enam Shah Alam for several years before successfully enrolling at UiTM Rembau, Negeri Sembilan.
